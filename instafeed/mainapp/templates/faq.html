What is Instafeed?

Instafeed is a social media aggregation site to help avoid the redundancy and headache of having to check/post to all your favorite social media sites. We aim to make a product that provides easy access to all the latest updates and an easy way to spread your message from one interface. Instafeed is for anyone who wants to simplify their social media experience. We believe that many people have become overwhelmed with the number of popular social media websites out there today and want to provide a tool that would give a better social media user experience.

How do I get started

  1. Click sign up to create a new Instafeed account.
  2. Click accounts to start adding your social media accounts
  3. Start posting and viewing feeds
